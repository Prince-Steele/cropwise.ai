FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Why did my crop recommendation change?

Recommendations respond to the season, farm profile data, recent crop history, and live weather context.

What should I do if a page stays in loading state?

Retry the page first. If the issue continues, note the page name and time, then contact support.

Can I trust the forecast as a guarantee?

No. Forecasts are planning aids only and should always be reviewed against field conditions and market realities.

How often should I update my farmer profile?

Update it when land size changes, you complete a harvest, switch crops, or move to a new operating area.

What helps support resolve my issue faster?

Send your account email, the page you were on, what action you took, and a screenshot if available.

Where can I ask for a product improvement?

Use the Contact Us page and mark the request as product feedback so it routes to the right team.

Checklist

Before contacting support

  1. Refresh the page and retry the action once.
  2. Confirm your farm details and selected crop are correct.
  3. Capture the page name, timestamp, and a screenshot of the issue.
