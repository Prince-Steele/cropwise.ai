Policy

Privacy Policy

Cropwise is built to help farmers make better operational decisions. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, how we protect it, and what choices you have.

At a glance
  • We collect account, farm, and usage data needed to operate the platform.
  • We use that data to generate advice, improve reliability, and support users.
  • You can contact us to request updates or ask privacy questions.
Collection

Information we collect

  • Account details such as your name, email, phone number, and login activity.
  • Farm profile details including location, land size, crop history, and preferences.
  • Content you submit while using support or advisory features.
  • Technical usage data that helps us maintain uptime, security, and bug tracking.
Use

How we use information

  • To deliver recommendations, forecasts, and profile-driven platform features.
  • To troubleshoot issues and respond to support requests.
  • To improve accuracy, interface quality, and service performance over time.
  • To detect misuse, protect accounts, and maintain service integrity.
Sharing and retention

How data is handled

  • We may use service providers that help with hosting, analytics, communications, or support operations.
  • We do not sell your personal data to third parties.
  • We keep information only as long as reasonably needed for service delivery, legal compliance, or dispute handling.
  • We apply security controls intended to protect account and platform data, but no system is risk free.
Your choices

Access, updates, and questions

  • You can update your farmer profile and account details inside the platform.
  • You can contact us to ask privacy questions or request support with your account information.
  • If this policy changes materially, we may update the page and revise the published effective date.
Contact

Privacy questions

Email privacy@cropwise.app if you have questions about this policy or how your information is handled.
