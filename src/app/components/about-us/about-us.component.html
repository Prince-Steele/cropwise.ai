About Cropwise

Cropwise helps farmers make clearer planting, forecasting, and market decisions with practical tools designed for real production conditions across Jamaica and the wider Caribbean.

What we focus on
Planting Timing and crop fit
Forecasting Yield and season planning
Markets Better pricing awareness
Built for the field

What the platform is meant to do

Guide crop selection

We help farmers compare options using farm profile details, seasonality, and practical crop signals.

Improve planning

Cropwise turns scattered data points into clearer forecasts that support production and harvest timing.

Support selling decisions

Market visibility helps users understand where demand may be stronger before they commit acreage.

Work for more than one user type

The platform is useful for producers, advisors, buyers, and agriculture programs coordinating at scale.

Our approach

Practical decision support, not hype

Cropwise is built around the idea that better farm decisions come from combining local context, structured inputs, and decision support that is easy to act on.

The platform does not replace field knowledge. It is designed to strengthen it by helping users compare options faster, spot risk earlier, and make planning decisions with more confidence.

Principles

How we think about the product

  • Keep recommendations understandable enough to review before acting.
  • Design for real farm operations, not lab-perfect assumptions.
  • Use data to support judgment, not to pretend uncertainty is gone.
  • Build trust by being transparent about what the platform can and cannot do.
Who we serve

Primary users

  • Farmers planning acreage, crop mix, and harvest cycles.
  • Agricultural advisors supporting better on-farm decisions.
  • Buyers and program teams looking for clearer production insight.
