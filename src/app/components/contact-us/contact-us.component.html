Faster support

What to include in your message

  • Your account email or phone number.
  • The page or tool where the issue happened.
  • The crop, acreage, or weather context involved.
  • A screenshot or short description of what you expected to happen.
  • The date and approximate time the issue occurred.
Escalation flow

How requests move through the team

  1. Support reviews the request and confirms the issue scope.
  2. Product or engineering receives anything that needs a deeper fix.
  3. You receive an update with either a resolution or next step.
